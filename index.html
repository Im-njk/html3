Low Cholesterol Diet : How to Decrease Your Cholesterol Level Faster

67890

13-Min Read

Cholesterol Is The Fatty, Waxy Substance That Our Liver Makes To Perform Some Of The Important Body Functions.

Cholesterol Is Important To Make Vitamin D, Some Hormones Like Estrogen, Progesterone And Androgens Etc.

While An Optimum Amount Of Cholesterol Is Needed For Some Important Body Functions And Creating The Cell Membrane As Well, A High Cholesterol Level May Create Some Problems Like A Deposition In The Blood Vessels Leading To Cardiovascular Diseases And Stroke.

As The Sources Of Cholesterol Are Two, The Dietary Sources And The Liver, So Cutting Down On Fatty Food And Switching To A Low-Cholesterol Diet Is The Best Measure.

This Article Throws Light On The Types Of Cholesterol, How To Control It And The Best Diet To Lower Cholesterol Levels Quickly.

Cholesterol and Its Role in the Body

The Main Functions Of Cholesterol In The Body Are The Formation Of Steroid Hormones, Vitamin D And The Creation Of Cell Membranes.

These Functions Are Essential As The Whole Reproductive Cycle Is Based On Steroid Hormones, Estrogen, Progesterone And Androgens.

Glucocorticoids And Mineralocorticoids Are Formed By Using Cholesterol As Well. Vitamin D Is Vital For Optimal Bone Health.

On The Other Hand, High Cholesterol Levels May Pose Some Problem In The Body Such As LDL Or Bad Cholesterol Is Responsible For The Deposition Of Cholesterol In Your Body.

HDL Or Good Cholesterol Carries Cholesterol From All Parts Of The Body To The Liver, So That It May Be Broken Down And Removed. HDL Is Doing Good To The Body By Removing Excess Cholesterol That's Why It's Known As Good Cholesterol And Vice Versa.

Benifits of low Cholesterol Diet

Reduced Risk Of Heart Diseases - A Low-Cholesterol, Low-Fat Diet Ensures The Reduction In LDL Or Bad Cholesterol. That's Why The Heart Works At Good Efficiency And The Risk Of Heart Disease Decreases Many Folds.

Weight Reduction -A Low-Cholesterol Diet Is Also Fiber-Rich, Easily Digestible And Low-Fat In Nature, This Leads To Weight Loss In Obese Individuals While Decreasing Cholesterol Levels As Well. It's An Absolute Win-Win For Obese Or Overweight People. . Better Physical Activity - When The Heart Is Pumping Blood At A Good Efficiency And Your Body Is Fit Then The Physical Activity Is Easy To Execute.

Prevention Of Atherosclerosis - Atherosclerosis Is The Deposition Of Plaque In The Arteries, This Narrows The Pathway Thereby Increasing The Risk Of Stroke And Other Cardiovascular Disorders (Source).

Blood Pressure Regulation - When There Is No Narrowing Of Arteries Then The Blood Flows Normally Within Them Resulting In Normal Blood Pressure.

How a Low Cholesterol Diet Works

Cutting Down On Saturated And Trans Fats Like Deep-Fried Food, Processed Food, Some Meat, Full-Fat Dairy, Butter Or Margarine Etc May Stop The Production Of LDL Or Bad Cholesterol In The Body.

Consuming Unsaturated Fats In Moderation Improves The Amount Of HDL In The Blood Which Accelerates The Removal Of Excess Cholesterol From The Blood Thereby Lowering Cholesterol Levels.

Conclusion

To Prevent The Effects Of High Cholesterol On The Heart, Brain And Body, Following A Cholesterol-Lowering Diet Is The Best Option.

Adding Nuts, Nut Butter, Cruciferous Vegetables, Omega-3 Rich Foods, Citrus Fruits, Dark Chocolate And Soya Products Have Been Proven To Reduce The Bad Cholesterol Or LDL Levels Effectively And Increase The Good Cholesterol Levels (HDL) As Well.

Good Lifestyle Changes And A Low Cholesterol Diet May Be The Key To A Healthy Heart And Healthy Body.
